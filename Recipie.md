The steps of chocolate brownie are:-
  

 1) Mix cocoa Powder with milk in a bowl (whisk 2-3 min)
 2) Add eggs,suggar & vanilla essance to your mixture (whisk 2-3 min)
 3)Finally add baking soda to it (whisk 2-3 min)
 4)Add it to a oven tray
 5)Add a nice layer of hersheys to it (Dont wisk it)
 6)Finally put the tray in the Oven
 7)Put the oven on 180 degree F for  30 min
 8)After 30 mins take the trey out garnish it again with some Hersheys cut it into pieces and ENJOY!!!!!!
